Hi, I'm Dan!


I'm a Product designer, design systems aficionado, front end developer, and chocolate enjoyer.


I studied Interactive Media Design at the University of Washington where I built a strong foundation in user research, rapid iteration, and accessibility best practices.


My journey in design began in 2012 with graphic design, and transitioned to product design in 2023.


Some notable career achievements and fun facts of mine are:

- I've created a guided onboarding tool for a fortune 500 company.
- I support the design system for a fortune 500 company.
- I've completed over 200 design requests within 6 months.
- I've contributed to dozens of pages of accessibility documentation.
- I independently designed, developed and deployed a UX writing tool to help PMs.
- I've designed and programmed 5 small indie video games, one of which earned me 1st place in the 2023 University of Washington Game Jam.
- I've coded dozens of responsive websites, including the portfolio website you're watching this video on right now.


From Figma design and animated prototyping, to front-end development and stakeholder management, I help bring ideas to life.


I’m always looking for new ways to grow as a designer. If you’re working on something cool, let me know!
